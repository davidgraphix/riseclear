# RiseClear Property Services — Website

A premium, conversion-focused website for **RiseClear Property Services** in Winnipeg, Manitoba, Canada. Built with Next.js 14 (App Router), TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, and Framer Motion.


## 📱 Features

- ✅ **Sticky navbar** with mobile hamburger menu
- ✅ **Smooth scroll** navigation
- ✅ **Hero section** with high-impact window cleaning focus
- ✅ **Trust section** with 4 key selling points + stats
- ✅ **Services section** with featured Window Cleaning card + 6 service cards
- ✅ **Process section** — 3-step how it works
- ✅ **Benefits section** — with floating animated image card
- ✅ **Testimonials** — 6 realistic Winnipeg client reviews
- ✅ **CTA section** — strong conversion section
- ✅ **Contact form** — with loading/success/error states
- ✅ **Nodemailer** — sends email to business + auto-reply to client
- ✅ **Floating WhatsApp button** — with popup card and pre-filled message
- ✅ **Mobile-first responsive design**
- ✅ **SEO optimized** — meta tags, OpenGraph, structured data (JSON-LD)
- ✅ **Framer Motion animations** — entrance animations, floating elements
- ✅ **Dark premium design** — with blue accents and glassmorphism
